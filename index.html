How savings are calculated

Annual hours saved = (Time without AI − Time with AI) × Times per year

Annual CAD saved = Annual hours saved × $105.10/hr — the blended fully-loaded hourly rate used across TELUS Digital for productivity calculations.

Build cost = Build time (hrs) × $105.10/hr — a one-time investment. The Year 1 net subtracts this from the annual savings to show true first-year ROI. In subsequent years, the full annual savings apply.

% time saved = (Time without AI − Time with AI) ÷ Time without AI
